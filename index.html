Training Modules

NeuroTouch offers a comprehensive suite of training scenarios covering essential neurosurgical procedures

Tumor Resection

Practice removing brain tumors with varying properties (consistency, vascularity, location) while preserving surrounding healthy tissue. The system provides real-time feedback on resection percentage, damage to critical structures, and blood loss.

Learn More →

Hemostasis Training

Learn to manage intraoperative bleeding through various techniques such as bipolar coagulation, application of hemostatic agents, and suction usage.

Learn More →

Endoscopic Ventricular Procedures

Develop skills in navigating ventricular spaces using endoscopic tools for procedures such as third ventriculostomy and colloid cyst removal.

Learn More →
